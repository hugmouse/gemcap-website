Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

Introduction

Gemcap ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. And me – yes, me, the creator of the Gemcap – too. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use in the Gemcap browser application for Android.

TLDR

We don't collect anything and you can always review the source code available here.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information or user data.

Gemcap is designed with privacy as a core principle. The application does not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Track your browsing history or activity
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Contain advertisements or third-party trackers
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device data

Local Data Storage

All user data, including bookmarks, browsing history, identities, and preferences, are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Remains on your device at all times
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Can be cleared at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the application

Third-Party Content

Gemcap is a browser application that allows you to access content on Geminispace (the Gemini protocol network). We are not responsible for:

  • The content you access through the browser
  • Any user-generated content visible on Gemini capsules
  • The privacy practices of websites or capsules you visit
  • Data collection by third-party Gemini servers

Open Source

Gemcap is an open-source application licensed under the Apache License 2.0. The source code is publicly available on GitHub, allowing anyone to verify our privacy claims and inspect how the application works.

Permissions

The app may request the following Android permissions:

  • Internet access: Required to browse Gemini capsules
  • Storage access: Required to save bookmarks and local data on your device

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and not for data collection or tracking.

Children's Privacy

Gemcap does not collect any data from users, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, there is no risk to children's privacy from using this application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository at github.com/hugmouse/gemcap
